Fidelity > Efficiency

"We must prioritize the fidelity of the learning process over the efficiency of the product."

‚ùå

The Efficiency Trap

Using AI to quickly write lesson plans or grade essays without human review. It's faster, but disconnects the teacher from the student and bypasses the cognitive struggle that builds understanding.

‚úÖ

The Fidelity Focus

Using AI to create role-play scenarios that deepen critical thinking. It might take more time to design, but the learning is deeper and students develop genuine mastery.

Redefining "Cheating"

Stop playing whack-a-mole with detectors

The Old Lens

"Students are using AI to cheat on the essay. We need detection software and stricter bans."

The Problem with Detectors:

  • ‚Ä¢ 98% false positive rate for non-native English speakers
  • ‚Ä¢ Already obsolete with new models
  • ‚Ä¢ Creates adversarial student relationships
Source: Stanford HAI Study ‚Üó

The Intention Lens

"If an AI can complete the assignment with high quality and zero human input, the assignment is no longer a valid measure of learning."

The Frontier Reframe:

  • ‚Ä¢ Assess the process, not just the product
  • ‚Ä¢ Design tasks requiring human judgment
  • ‚Ä¢ Use AI as thinking partner, not replacement

Example Redesign:

Old Assignment

"Write a 5-paragraph essay on the causes of WWI"

‚Üí AI can do this in 30 seconds

Redesigned Assignment

"Use AI to generate 3 different historical perspectives on WWI. Identify the bias in each. Write your synthesis of what actually happened and why the narratives differ."

‚Üí AI is the tool, critical thinking is the skill

Using the Framework

The CoSN framework is flexible by design. Districts can assess themselves based on their organizational structure and priorities.

Who Assesses What?

Each domain should be assessed by the leaders who own that area of responsibility. This ensures accurate self-evaluation and accountability for improvement.

‚Üí
Technology leadership evaluates technical and security readiness
‚Üí
Legal/HR/Business assess risk, policy, and operational domains
‚Üí
Instructional leaders evaluate academic literacy and curriculum integration
‚Üí
Executive leadership assesses vision, strategy, and governance

The Value of Honesty

Most districts will score "Emerging" in multiple domains. That's expected and okay.

The goal isn't to achieve "Mature" status immediately. The goal is to:

1. Know where you actually are
2. Identify your highest risks
3. Create a realistic path forward

"You can't improve what you don't measure. And you can't measure what you're not willing to honestly assess."

After the Assessment

Once you've identified your gaps, you can use AI to help synthesize your findings and create an actionable roadmap that's specific to your district's needs and resources.
